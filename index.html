I'm the Co-Founder and CEO of Montra, a slide based video creation tool that makes it easy to create professional videos.

Born in Toronto, Ontario, I got my start producing videos for the CBC (Canadian Broadcasting Corporation) when I was fourteen. A year later, inspired by the world of startups, I launched my own podcast, interviewing tech investors and entrepreneurs including Mark Cuban, Chris Sacca, Scott Galloway, and the founders of Figma, Headspace and Axios to name a few.

In 2021, my TikTok account (which started as a place to share clips from my podcast) was acquired by Workweek.

That following year, I took on various freelance video creation projects for startups including Levels Health. This work ultimately served as the inspiration for what would become Montra.

We're building a video creation tool for software companies at Montra, and after spending the last decade tinkering with nearly every video editor under the sun, it's fitting that I'm finally building my own. You can check in on our progress here.

I'm currently living in Brooklyn, New York, where I like to make videos, listen to podcasts and work out of coffee shops.
